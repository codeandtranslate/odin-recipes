Pesto Pasta Salad

With red capsicum, vegetarian salami and an authentic simple pesto dressing
a.k.a the perfect lunch for a long hot mid-summer day at the office

Ciao raggazi! One of the best dishes to get you cool, focused and back on track for the rest of the day is this timeless summer classic from Bella Italia :

Simple to prepare, easy to cook and bloody refreshing to eat.
A classic which you should make a staple lunch in your two week rotation.

All you need is:

  • 2x fresh red capsicums from the supermarket
  • 1x 500g pack of pasta
  • 1x jar of pesto
  • 2x packets of your favourite (vegetarian) salami
  • 1x pack of feta
  • 1x pack of rocket
